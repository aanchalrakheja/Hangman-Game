RULES

  • The aim of the player is to guess the word by hit and trial method.

  • The player will have to guess a character from the given virtual board. If the character is a part of the word to be guessed, it will be placed at its respective position.

  • If the character chosen by the player is not a part of the word to be guessed, the the player will lose a life and the hangman will keep on building.

  • The player has 6 lives until the Hangman is completely built

  • The player wins if he/she succeeds in guessing the word before losing all 6 lives i.e before the whole hangman is created and fails otherwise.
